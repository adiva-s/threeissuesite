Where to Donate

Islamic Relief USA - Turkey

In Turkey, the main focus in ensuring fmailies receive cash assistance to pay for aid such as food,
water, medicine. Turkey is also home to many refugees of which most are Syrian. Islamic Relief
intends to also provide relief for refugees as well.


Islamic Relief USA - Syria

Due to more than a decade in war, more than thousands of people have died in Syria.
The standard of living has been set back. "Roughly 6.9 million people are displaced
within Syria while 6.8 million have been forced out of their country." Most of Syria
lives in poverty and has lost basic needs such as access to food and clean
water. Islamic Relief's focus is providing food, clean water, medicine to the people of Syria.


UNICEF USA

UNICEF teams are on ground, helping out any way they can. Their top priority
is ensuring that children are safe and secure with proper shelter, along with reuniting
them with their family. The teams ensure their basic needs are met
- food, water, sanitation, winter clothes and medical care.


GlobalGiving

All donations will support relief and recovery efforts by first aiding first responders in meeting
the victim's needs for food, medicine, and sheleter. Additionally,
the donations will provide psycho-social and mental health support to the survivors.


OXFAM

OXFAM is an intenational organization that is said to be working with 80 women's cooperatives
in Turkish provinces most affected by the earthquake to determine an appropiate response
plan. They aim to provide protection, water, sanitation, shelter, food support and long term rehabiliation and reconstruction.
