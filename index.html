Syria & Turkey
Earthquake

Syria and Turkey, already struggling with decades of wars and refugee crises
were struck by a 7.8 earthquake on February 6, 2023. Hours later, a 7.7 earthquake
strikes Turkey. Following both earthquakes were at least 2,100 aftershocks.
Since then the death tolls has steadily increased to at least 47,905 people. More and more deaths are found with every passing day.
